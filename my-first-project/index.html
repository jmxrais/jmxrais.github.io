the albums

brat and it's the same but there's three more songs so it's not

charli xcx

this is my album of the year. not the first, not the remix album. this one.

this is an iconic pop album. brat has set the ethos of the 2020s: it is a great resume of the post-pandemic party scene and equally the mentally disthrot gen z is under. it is the end of the world and we don't know whether we party or plan on having children, while we wonder whether we make any difference to the people around us. also, drugs.

available on spotify


maria esmeralda

thalin feat. cravinhos feat. VCR slim

this is a great brazilian album. it is a hard album to classify because it merges rap, hip-hop, MPB and other genres, creating something that could be extremely chaotic - but isn't. the use of sampling is precise, yet unavoidable. it is an album with a story, but also one that opens up to many other narratives and sounds.

i like it very much.

available on spotify

imaginal disk

magdalena bay

this is a great pop album. i'd qualify it as a dream pop album because the vocals are rather otherwordly and the lyrics are somewhat dreamy, yet they remain relatable. the sound production is delicate yet complete, but not overwhelming.

this is not usually my genre but i like it very much.

available on spotify
